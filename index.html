UGC Limiteds

Browse UGC limited items. New UGC limiteds are added here shortly after they are released on Roblox.

UGC data comes from the same /items/v1/itemdetails endpoint under the ugc_limiteds property. Each item is a flat array: [id, name, acronym, rap, value, default_value, demand, trend, projected, hyped, rare]
